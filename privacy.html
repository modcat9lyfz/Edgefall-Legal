Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

I. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what data Edgefall, developed and operated by Sol ("we", "us"), collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data. We are committed to handling your information responsibly.

II. Data We Collect

When you interact with Edgefall, we collect and store the following information:

  • Discord User ID — a unique identifier assigned to your Discord account, used to link your game data to your account
  • Discord Username — your username at the time of account creation, used for display purposes
  • Game Data — your in-game progress, stats, inventory, activity history, and any other data generated through gameplay

We do not collect passwords, email addresses, payment information, or any other personal data beyond what is listed above.

III. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to operate and improve Edgefall. Specifically:

  • To store and retrieve your game progress and stats
  • To display your username in leaderboards, profiles, and game events
  • To enforce game rules and detect abuse or cheating
  • To improve gameplay systems and balance

We do not sell, share, or disclose your data to any third parties for commercial purposes.

IV. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on a secured private server. We take reasonable precautions to protect your data from unauthorised access. However, no system is entirely immune to breaches, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

V. Patreon

If you support Edgefall via Patreon, any payment and billing data is handled exclusively by Patreon. We only receive confirmation of your supporter status to apply in-game perks. We do not have access to your payment details.

VI. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as you use Edgefall. If you wish to have your data deleted, you may request this by contacting us using the details below. Upon a valid deletion request, we will remove your data within a reasonable timeframe.

VII. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

VIII. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of Edgefall after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
